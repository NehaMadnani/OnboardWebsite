Flame - Your Onboarding Assistant

Optimise the Onboarding Experience for Engineers
The engineering onboarding process often proves time-consuming, lacking clarity, and heavily reliant on mentor-mentee dynamics. We leverage technology and thoughtful planning to make the journey efficient and straightforward for everyone involved.

Accelerate Your First Commit

Streamline your onboarding journey with our structured approach, reducing the time it takes to make that crucial first commit.

Save Mentor Hours

We provide clarity on when to meet in person, ensuring efficient mentorship without unnecessary hours spent. Focus on what matters most - guiding your engineers to success.

Empower Managers with Insight

Gain a clear overview of your team's onboarding progress. Our tool aids in making informed 6-month and 1-year plans, allowing you to strategize and manage resources effectively.
